support immigrant families and their rights

Join us Friday and Saturday (July 6-7) at the ART for ACLU fundraiser in support of immigrant families and meet our new executive director, Alanah Odoms Hebert.

Louisianans all across the state are rallying against the Trump administration's cruel treatment of immigrants, and this week there's a way to support local artists in NOLA and immigrant families at the same time.

Brand New Orleans Art Gallery will be hosting the fundraiser and donating their gallery profits directly to the ACLU to support immigrant families and their rights.

The fundraiser will take place on Friday and Saturday from 11 a.m. to 7 p.m., and you can meet our new executive director, Alanah Odoms Hebert, on Saturday from 4 to 5 p.m.

See you there!
