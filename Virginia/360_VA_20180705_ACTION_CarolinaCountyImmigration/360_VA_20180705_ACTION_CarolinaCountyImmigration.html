Let your elected Caroline County supervisors know they were wrong to sneak through approval of a contract allowing federal immigration agencies to detain immigrants at the former Peumansend Creek Regional Jail.

Residents of Caroline County had no opportunity to know what was happening or say whether they agree that the use of a facility owned by the public as county taxpayers should be used to enable the zero tolerance anti-immigration policy of the Trump administration.
