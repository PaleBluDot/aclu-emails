As a new year begins, we want to share our deep gratitude and appreciation for you – our community of supporters who make this work possible – and say thank you for all you did to help advance Vermonters' civil rights and liberties last year.

To see how your support made a difference in 2020, please read our Impact Report.

Read our Impact Report

In the midst of multiple historical crises, you helped us pass sweeping criminal law reforms, part of an effort to invest in people over prisons that has resulted in hundreds of fewer Vermonters being incarcerated. You helped us win protections for Vermont immigrant communities targeted by ICE and Border Patrol. You helped us advance transformative police reforms, giving Vermont the best use of force and facial surveillance bans in the country.

That's to say nothing of the nationwide ACLU victories in defense of voting rights, reproductive freedom, racial justice, and LGBTQ rights – you were a part of those critically important efforts also.

As we turn the page on 2020, we hope you will take a moment to celebrate these and other ACLU of Vermont victories.

Heading into the new year, we are not backing down – we're doubling down. No matter what 2021 holds in store, our mission remains unchanged – to realize the promise of justice and equality for all people, particularly those from whom that promise has been withheld. This year and every year, you can count on us to keep fighting for a more perfect union – one that ensures we the people really does include all of us.

On behalf of everyone at ACLU, we wish you a happy new year and thank you for everything your support makes possible.
