NATIONAL BOOK TOUR COMING TO NEW ORLEANS

Over the past year, thanks to your support, we have delivered on our promise to challenge President Trump's unconstitutional and divisive policies – in the courts, in the state capitol, and in communities across Louisiana.

To mark the anniversary of President Trump's inauguration, a new anthology of stories and art is being published to benefit the ACLU – and the national book tour is coming right here to New Orleans.

Join us on Tuesday, January 30 at Octavia Books for a reading and presentation of "It Occurs to Me That I Am America: New Stories and Art" with author Tom Piazza and editor Jonathan Santlofer.
