With COVID-19 again on the rise in Indiana, we know this pandemic is having a serious impact on our democracy and posing significant barriers to the ballot. But no Hoosier should have to choose between their health and their right to vote.

Indiana elected officials must take action now to ensure that Hoosiers have the option to vote absentee in November. We are urging Indiana Election Officials to immediately enact critical changes for the upcoming election.

Changes should include enacting no-excuse absentee voting, prolonging the deadline to submit the application, extending the deadline to return absentee ballots, and ensuring that Hoosiers can vote in-person if needed.

We must act now to make it as safe and easy as possible for Hoosier voters to cast their ballots in the upcoming election.
