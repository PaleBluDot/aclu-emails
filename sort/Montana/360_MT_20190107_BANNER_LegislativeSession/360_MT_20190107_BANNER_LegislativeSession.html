Today marks the start of the 2019 Montana State Legislative Session. Rest assured, we are out in force to safeguard the civil rights and civil liberties of all Montanans.

This legislative session we will work to end the criminalization of poverty, abolish the death penalty, and ensure Montanans’ reproductive rights, including those who are incarcerated.

As always, we will fight bills that restrict our First Amendment rights, dehumanize immigrants, chip away at reproductive freedom, threaten LGBTQ people, or disproportionately harm Indigenous people and other people of color.

Visit our website to learn about the specific bills we support or oppose.

We cannot succeed without your help! Your legislators are most influenced by you — their constituents. Stay informed and be ready to act. Get our updates and action alerts: sign-up at aclumontana.org and follow us on Facebook, Twitter, and Instagram. Your voice makes a difference!

Want to do more? Mark your calendars for the ACLU of Montana Lobby Day. We hope you will join us on Feb. 11 in Helena to learn about effective lobbying strategies and tell your legislators what issues are important to you. Can’t make it? Check out our online toolkit to stay engaged.

Thanks for making your voice heard during the legislative session and beyond.
