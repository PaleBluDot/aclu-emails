Join us and partnered organizations as we march in support and celebration of the LGBTQ community at the 2018 Chicago Pride Parade!

In June, we remember the strides we have achieved for LGBTQ equality, but as the recent debate around the Masterpiece Cakeshop case before the Supreme Court shows, the battle for equality is not over. The attacks on students who are transgender — and efforts to deny students like Gavin Grimm in Virginia and Nova Maday in Illinois the basic dignity of being able to use the appropriate restroom and locker room — make clear that we must stand with the LGBTQ community to protect our gains and continue working towards full equality.

We will be marching in Chicago's Pride Parade this year, and we'd love to have you join us! The 2018 Chicago Pride Parade is on Sunday, June 24. Sign up here to march with us and our partner organizations. We will have shirts, posters, fans and more on a first come basis.
