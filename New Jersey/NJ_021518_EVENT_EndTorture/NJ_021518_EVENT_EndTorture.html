In New Jersey's prisons and jails, torture is an everyday occurrence. Solitary confinement is used routinely as a form of punishment, despite widespread knowledge of its damaging effects.

Join us on Sunday, February 25, to hear stories of solitary confinement survivors and learn why it's so urgent for New Jersey to stop the routine use of this brutal punishment.

The event is free, and it will bring together survivors of solitary, advocates, and people in our communities concerned about the well-being of all New Jerseyans.

We hope to see you there!
