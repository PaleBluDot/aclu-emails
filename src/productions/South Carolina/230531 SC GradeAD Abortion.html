Last week, an extreme abortion ban was signed into law. Thankfully, Planned Parenthood South Atlantic, Greenville Women's Clinic, and two physicians have filed a lawsuit that seeks to block this unconstitutional ban. On Friday, an injunction was granted, the six-week ban has been blocked for now, and abortion remains legal until 21 weeks in South Carolina.

So, where do we go from here?

We start by remembering that no matter how hard the fight to protect our rights can be, we are never in it alone. In the past few weeks, hundreds of people took action with us. You called key Senators, contacted Representatives, and even canvassed in key communities. We didn't get the outcome we worked for, but I know your voices were heard loud and clear.

Next, we organize. We are bringing Legislative Look Back events to communities around the state in June. At these events, we'll look back at the legislative session and discuss what we can do to prepare for the work ahead.
