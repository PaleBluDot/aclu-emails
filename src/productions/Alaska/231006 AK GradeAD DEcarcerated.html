DEcarcerated

DEcarcerated is an event hosted by the ACLU of Alaska to humanize individuals who have interacted with the criminal legal system, and to create space for those who are incarcerated to be part of the community. This evening hosts artists, storytellers, local food, and music to spread a message of unity and rebellion of a system that requires rectification.
