Civil Rights Frontier: Transgender Rights in Alaska

One of the nation's most renowned transgender civil rights activists will speak tonight Tuesday, August 29 in Anchorage iat a FREE public education event about the national fight over transgender non-discrimination protections and how Proposition 1 puts Anchorage front and center in that fight.

Civil Rights Frontier: Transgender Rights in Alaska
Tuesday, August 29, 7 - 8:45 p.m.
Wendy Williamson Auditorium
University of Alaska-Anchorage
2533 Providence Drive
Anchorage, AK 99508
RSVP with Fair Anchorage

Mara Keisling, executive director of the National Center for Transgender Equality, has been a leading voice in advancing significant policy victories for transgender people on the federal and state levels. She will share her thoughts on the state of transgender rights in Alaska as well as lessons learned about the harm repealing Anchorage's transgender non-discrimination protections would mean for the city's reputation and our transgender neighbors and friends. The evening will end with transgender people and parents of transgender children in Anchorage discussing their concerns about the impact of Proposition 1.

This is a FREE event and free parking will also be available. You will not want to miss out as one of the country's most prominent transgender advocates joins us to discuss Proposition 1.

See you tonight!
