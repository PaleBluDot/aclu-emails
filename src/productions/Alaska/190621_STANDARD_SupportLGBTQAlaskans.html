Dear ##SM_RULE_360 Email Salutation##,

Pride celebrations are happening all over Alaska and we couldn’t be more thrilled to be there to support our LGBT friends and neighbors.

We need to ensure LGBT Alaskans are fully protected under state law.

Lesbian, gay, bisexual, and transgender people in Alaska continue to face discrimination in their daily lives. A 2015 statewide survey of transgender Alaskans found 32 percent of adults had experienced housing discrimination, and 25 percent suffered employment discrimination — from being forced to use the wrong restroom, to being told to present in the wrong gender in order to keep their job.

Help put an end to this kind of discrimination. Demand your state representative and state senator co-sponsor non-discrimination legislation!

Regardless of whether you can make it out to a Pride event over the next two weeks (you really should, they’re awesome!), take a minute to help make a measurable impact on the lives of LGBT Alaskans by telling your legislators to step up and co-sponsor legislation providing the LGBT community equal protection under state law.

Stop by one of our booths at Talkeetna Pride, the 5th annual Rainbow Run, or Anchorage Pride Festival and Equality Parade. We have great games, beautiful ACLU branded merch to give away, and important information on how you can protect and assert your rights.

In liberty,

Casey Reynolds
Communications Director, ACLU of Alaska
