Few individuals have had such a dramatic and lasting impact on the expansion and protection of our rights than Supreme Court Justice Ruth Bader Ginsburg. From her time as the founding director of the ACLU's Women's Rights Project obtaining groundbreaking decisions from the Supreme Court to the landmark decisions she authored on the federal bench and Supreme Court, Justice Ginsburg dedicated her life to the realization of the Constitution's guarantee of equal protection under the law.

Join us this Wednesday, September 30, at 12 p.m. CST / 11 a.m. MST, to memorialize and honor the life and legacy of Justice Ruth Bader Ginsburg at our live online event, Honoring RBG Through Action.
