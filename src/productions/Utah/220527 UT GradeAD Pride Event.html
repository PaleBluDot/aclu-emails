Pride is a protest and a celebration of our LGBTQ+ community, and we need you to join us! In a moment of increased attacks on our fundamental rights and our LGBTQ+ youth, we must unite in solidarity. That's why we're very excited to be participating in this year's Utah Pride Festival and Parade.

You can be an ambassador for the ACLU of Utah and help us show up for Pride by marching with us at the parade or volunteering at our booth at the Pride Festival!

We have several shifts available for volunteering opportunities; feel free to sign up for as many shifts as you want:

We will do a short training on being an ACLU ambassador during this event, tie-dye our t-shirts (t-shirts and all materials provided), and make pride buttons to hand out! Festival volunteers will pick up their tickets at this event.

The benefit of volunteering: You will get a free t-shirt (tie-dye decorating party details above!). Festival shift volunteers will also get a free ticket to enjoy the Pride Festival at any point in the day before or after your shift. Shift openings and tickets are limited, so sign up today!

Sign up here today and we'll show up together as an ACLU of Utah family to support and celebrate our LGBTQ+ community here in Utah!
