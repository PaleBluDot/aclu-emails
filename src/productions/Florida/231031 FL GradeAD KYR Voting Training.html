Considering the changes made to Florida's election laws earlier this year, it is crucial to learn how these revisions can impact you and your eligibility to vote. We are seeing more measures pass across the country that make it harder for Americans—particularly Black people, the elderly, students, and people with disabilities—to exercise their fundamental right to cast a ballot. To protect our democracy, we must learn how to combat voter suppression in our communities.

Join us on Thursday, November 2, 7 p.m. for our Know Your Voting Rights Training. Members of our Let Florida Vote! Campaign will share updates and important voting rights information. The training will cover topics such as changes made to the vote-by-mail process, tips for voting with a disability, and how to report problems at the polls.
