Join us on Friday, April 27 for Civil Cafe: Demanding Gender Equality, presented by Civil Beat in partnership with the ACLU of Hawai'i and the YWCA O'ahu.

Female athletes in many Hawai'i public schools aren't getting the equality that federal law promises them under Title IX. A recent story by Honolulu Civil Beat exposed stark gender inequities when it comes to high school athletics programs and facilities. Join us this Friday for a community conversation highlighting this particular example, which prompted us to issue a demand letter to the state Department of Education requesting that female athletes in public schools have access to the same facilities as male athletes.

Panelist include:

  • Khara Jabola-Carolus, executive director of Hawaii State Commission on the Status of Women
  • Suevon Lee, reporter at Civil Beat
  • Mackenzie Ozoa, high school junior
  • Joshua Wisch, executive director of ACLU of Hawaii

Space is limited, please RSVP. Fuller Hall is ADA accessible.

Sincerely,

ACLU of Hawai'i
