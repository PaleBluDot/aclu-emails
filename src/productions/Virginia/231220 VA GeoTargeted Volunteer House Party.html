These past few months have been an absolute whirlwind.

Because of the importance of the November elections, we did more civic engagement around elections than ever in our affiliate's history. With the help of volunteer advocates like you, we made phone calls, sent text messages, and knocked on doors to fight for our shared values in Henrico County and beyond.

Henrico residents showed up to the polls and voted their values, and our hard work paid off.

But the work isn't over. It is only just beginning.

Now comes the hard task of holding our elected officials accountable. That's why the ACLU of Virginia is inviting you to our first-ever Henrico house party on Saturday, January 6, 6 – 8 p.m.
