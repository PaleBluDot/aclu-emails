We're the newest members of the ACLU-KY staff team. We're working together to coordinate a massive get out the vote effort, so all eligible Kentucky voters participate in the election. We're focusing on connecting with the 175,000+ Kentuckians with past felony convictions whose voting rights were recently restored.

Are you ready for the election? Visit our Voter Information Center to:

  • Register to vote or update your name/address by October 5.
  • Request a mail-in absentee ballot by October 9.
  • View a sample ballot and know your rights when voting.
  • Join our Voter Education + Registration webinar on Thursday, October 1 to see how you can help get out the vote.
  • Find out if your voting rights were restored if you have a past felony conviction. It's free. It's online. All you need are your name and birthday.

Our democracy is on the line up and down the ballot this election. It will take all of us to build a better Kentucky and a more just United States.
