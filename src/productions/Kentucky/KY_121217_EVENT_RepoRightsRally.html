Rally for reproductive rights Thursday, January 4 in Frankfort!

The General Assembly will be in session and legislators are not going to waste any time before attempting to advance bills to rollback reproductive rights. In fact, a bill that would totally ban abortion in Kentucky has already been pre-filed.

Join the ACLU of Kentucky, Planned Parenthood Advocates, the Kentucky Health Justice Network, and our incredible allies for a day of action supporting women's health and reproductive rights. Now, more than ever we need to rise up and show the General Assembly that #WeAreKY and #WeWillNotGoBack.

For details about registration and transportation, be sure to RSVP on Facebook. Questions? Send us an email.

Can't make the rally? Have other civil liberties issues you'd like to be in touch with your legislators about? We've set up action alerts on our top priority areas: abortion access, protections for pregnant workers, justice reform, LGBTQ rights, religious refusals, and access to the ballot box. Email your legislators today!
