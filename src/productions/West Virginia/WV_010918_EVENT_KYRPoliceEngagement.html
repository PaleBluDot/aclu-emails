Join us on Martin Luther King Jr. Day, Monday, January 15, in Bluefield. Our Legal Director Jamie Lynn Crofts, will present a free interactive talk to help citizens better understand how to interact with law enforcement and know their rights when encountering law enforcement.

This talk will cover topics including your rights when stopped by a police officer on the street or in your car, when an officer comes to your home, and when you are detained or arrested and questioned. We will also discuss issues surrounding police brutality.

We hope to see you there!
