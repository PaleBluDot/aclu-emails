We're celebrating you!

Please join us on Monday, October 30 for the ACLU of Maine's Annual Meeting and Member Appreciation Night. The reception will begin at 5:30 p.m. with a cash bar and light hors d'oeuvres.

After the reception, stay for a special screening of "Loving," an inspiring film about ACLU clients Richard and Mildred Loving and their landmark 1967 Supreme Court case Loving v. Virginia, which overturned state bans on interracial marriage.

Note: parking is permitted anywhere in the lot after 5 p.m.

We look forward to seeing you there!

Sincerely,

All of us at the ACLU of Maine
