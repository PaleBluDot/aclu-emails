Online privacy is under attack. Last week, the Maine House voted to protect Mainers' online privacy when they voted to pass LD 1610. Unfortunately, the Senate voted to put corporate profits ahead of our privacy and voted AGAINST this important bill.

LD 1610 now goes back to the Senate for another vote, and we want to change their minds. Your senator, Joyce Maker, is one of the votes we need to secure.

Call Senator Maker and tell her to vote YES on LD 1610 to protect online privacy: 207-287-1505

We shouldn't have to choose between using the internet and keeping our personal information private.
