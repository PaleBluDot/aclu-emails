Happy Pride Month! We are so excited to celebrate the LGBTQ+ communities of Louisiana a little extra this month.

And we can't forget that the first #pride was a protest led by Black and Brown LGBTQ+ women – and we are happy to remember this history and in awe that it is still happening today. This month and every month, we honor their legacy by continuing the fight for LGBTQ+ rights.

Here are some of the events happening across our colorful Pelican state:

  • Cenla PRIDE Weekend 2022 on Saturday, June 4, in Alexandria
  • Field Gay 2022 on Saturday, June 4, in Shreveport
  • New Orleans Pride on June 9 - 12, in New Orleans
  • Official NOLA Pride Parade 2022 on Saturday, June 11, in New Orleans
  • Pride Acadiana Pride Celebration on Saturday, June 11, in Lafayette
  • Baton Rouge Pride Fest 2022 on Saturday, June 25, in Baton Rouge
  • Black Pride NOLA Meet & Greet on Friday, July 1, in New Orleans
  • Black & Excellence LGBTQ Award Show & Ball on Saturday, July 2, in New Orleans
  • "Culture" Black NOLA Pride Saturday Night on Sunday, July 3, in New Orleans
  • Southern Decadence on Thursday, September 1, in New Orleans
  • QUEST: LGBTQ+ Music & Arts Festival on Saturday, October 29, in Shreveport

Happy #PrideMonth, y'all!
