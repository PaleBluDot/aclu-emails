This is a virtual event. After you RSVP, you will receive the Zoom link for joining the event. Make sure to add the event to your calendar.

At the event, you and hundreds of other dedicated Marylanders will learn more about the implementation of police accountability boards and the issues we will be tackling this legislative session.

Whether you're a seasoned advocate or if you haven't engaged in grassroots advocacy directly yet but would like to support local coalition efforts across the state, this is a perfect opportunity to plug into the work. For over 90 years, the ACLU of Maryland has focused on empowering Marylanders to exercise their rights so that the law values and uplifts their humanity. A key part of how we do that is working to better our state so that Black, Indigenous, and People of Color can live freely and have their rights respected.
