Last year, after years of organizing and advocacy by people across Illinois, the legislature passed the Pretrial Fairness Act, so that no one in our state would be jailed while awaiting trial simply because they cannot afford to pay bail. Ending money bonds and focusing judges' pretrial decisions on risk instead of access to wealth will lead to improved community safety and make our pretrial system more fair. Before the new law is implemented in 2023, it is already under attack.

Join us for two upcoming events to learn more about the efforts to end money bonds in Illinois, and how to fight back against proposals that would gut the legislation and result in a pretrial system far more unjust than the current system.
