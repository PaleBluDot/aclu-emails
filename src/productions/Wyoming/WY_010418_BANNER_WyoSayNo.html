The Trump administration's deportation machine is ramping up – and they're looking to detain more people right here in Wyoming. One of the country's largest for-profit private prison companies, Management and Training Corporation (MTC), together with Immigration and Customs Enforcement (ICE), wants to build an immigration prison in Uinta County.

Using our criminal justice system for immigration purposes is costly and devastating to immigrants and their families.

We're launching a campaign to push back against construction of this new immigration detention center. Join the ACLU of Wyoming and Juntos Wyoming at our #WyoSayNo launch event on Saturday, January 13 to learn how you can take action to stop the immigration prison in Uinta County.
