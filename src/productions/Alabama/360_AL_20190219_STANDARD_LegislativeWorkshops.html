The Alabama legislative session will start on Tuesday, March 5. Come join us for a training and discussion as we prepare to fight for what's important to us in Alabama.

We’ll be in Montgomery tonight, Tuesday, February 19 at 5:30 p.m. This event is free, and there will be tacos from El Campacino! Find out more information on Facebook.

We’ll also be in Huntsville on Thursday, February 28 at 6:30 p.m. This event is free, and more details will be announced soon on Facebook.

We'll be taking about:

  • How to find and track bills.
  • How to effectively engage legislators.
  • Issues likely to come up this session.
  • Ways to share your story to advocate for change.

We want to bring as many people together as possible so we can learn how to engage with our elected officials, talk about what matters most to us, and use our collective power to build a better Alabama. Please share!

This is part of a series of events: Look for more events in Birmingham and Selma! Contact us at info@aclualabama.org if you have a group who wants us to come and talk.

Hope to see you there!
