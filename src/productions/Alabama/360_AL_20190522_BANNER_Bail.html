Dear ##SM_RULE_360 Email Salutation##,

The Legislature is at it again, trying to pass a bill that can potentially result in thousands of Alabamians being thrown in jail, before trial, at great cost to taxpayers and Alabama communities.

HB282 proposes a constitutional amendment that, if enacted, would significantly expand the number of people who can be denied bail, which will dramatically increase Alabama’s jail population.

People who remain in jail before trial are more likely to be convicted. Alabama’s prisons are already overcrowded, so much so that the Department of Justice has demanded that the state fix the issue. Instead our legislators are pushing a bill that will make matters worse.

The Senate Judiciary Committee needs to hear from you. They meet next week to discuss this bill, so time is running out. Send them a message NOW.
