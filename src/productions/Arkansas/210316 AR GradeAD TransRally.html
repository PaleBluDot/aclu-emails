The Arkansas General Assembly has launched an all-out attack against trans youth across our state, introducing numerous pieces of legislation to strip away their fundamental rights.

House Bill 1570 would ban medically necessary care for trans kids. Senate Bill 289 would allow medical providers to refuse to provide care for almost any reason, including on the basis of sexual orientation or gender identity. Senate Bill 354 would ban transgender students from school sports. Other proposed constitutional amendments seek to elevate religious liberty and conscience above all other rights.

The goal of all of these bills is to erase and exclude transgender Arkansans. These hateful pieces of legislation are pure discrimination through and through. Can we count on you to rally with us – along with our allies at Intransitive, the Center for Artistic Revolution, TWANG Anthology, and the Young Trans Women's Project – against these dangerous bills and let trans youth know they are not alone?
