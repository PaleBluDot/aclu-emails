The runoff elections for the primary and nonpartisan (judicial and school board) elections are Tuesday, June 21! It's important to vote in runoff elections, because they will determine who will sit on the Arkansas Supreme Court as well as who will be on the ballot for the general elections in November.

Please join us in exercising your voting rights by:

  • Checking your registration status.
  • Checking your voting location.

Also, remember that you can vote in the runoff even if you missed the primary in May. There are special considerations for people experiencing homelessness and those who have a past criminal conviction. If you are unable to stand in line to wait to vote due to a disability, flag this to a poll worker, so they can accommodate you by moving you to the front of the line.

If you run into any problems, or have questions, you can contact the nonpartisan, nonprofit Election Protection Hotline.
