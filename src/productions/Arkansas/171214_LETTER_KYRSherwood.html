Last month, something amazing happened in Arkansas – the city of Sherwood agreed to stop jailing people for being poor.

Because of a lawsuit brought by us and others, Sherwood will no longer jail people because they can't afford to pay their debts. This is what criminal justice reform looks like – and you helped make it happen.

Though this settlement marks a victory, Sherwood is only one city. Vulnerable people are still being trapped in a never-ending cycle of escalating debt and incarceration – with devastating human costs.
