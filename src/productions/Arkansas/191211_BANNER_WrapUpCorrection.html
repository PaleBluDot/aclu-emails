Dear ##SM_RULE_360 Email Salutation##,

As we close out our 50th anniversary year, we're more grateful than ever for the members, donors, supporters, and volunteers who make our work possible.

Since 2016, the number of card-carrying ACLU of Arkansas members has more than doubled, and we now have more than 9,000 members and supporters across the state.

To wrap up our golden anniversary, we've created an online hub of 50th anniversary highlights, including a retrospective of our accomplishments and blog posts by people who have fought with us on the front lines.

The site features blogs from civil rights champions like Rizelle Aaron, Representative Vivian Flowers, Nan Selz, Bettina Brownstein and Ernie Dumas, who all generously shared their reflections on a half-century of freedom fighting.

You can also read a timeline of key moments in ACLU of Arkansas history, from when we overturned a law requiring the teaching of "creation science" to when we defeated a ban on LGBTQ people fostering and adopting children.

We wouldn't have accomplished all that we have over the last 50 years without your support and the support of people like you.

As we wrap up our 50th anniversary year, we are more dedicated than ever to keeping up the fight for justice and liberty – and daring to create a more perfect union for all.

Thank you,

Holly Dickson
Interim Executive Director and Legal Director
ACLU of Arkansas
