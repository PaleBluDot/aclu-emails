Nationwide, anti-democratic efforts are gaining ground, and those with fascist views and tactics are feeling emboldened to undermine our fundamental rights and freedoms.

Anti-LGBTQ politicians and courts are blocking access to healthcare for transgender and pregnant people and rolling back protections for LGBTQ+ and BIPOC communities. Low-income people living on the streets are dehumanized, even here in progressive-leaning Oregon.

Our state is also facing record numbers of attempted bans on books authored by or about the LGBTQ+ community and people of color. We're also facing regressive efforts to restart the war on drugs by criminalizing drug addiction instead of addressing it as a health care matter.

Tune into the 2023 ACLU of Oregon Annual Meeting to hear about how we're pushing back and what you can do to be a part of the movement forward. Don't miss your chance to learn from Peppermint, ACLU's Artist Ambassador for Transgender Justice, who will be in conversation with ACLU of Oregon team member Jude al-Ghazal Stone.

Please note that this event will not be recorded.
