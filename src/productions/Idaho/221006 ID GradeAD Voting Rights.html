Election season is upon us, and it's time for us to work together. There's power in numbers. The right to vote and have our voices heard in our democracy is on the ballot in Idaho. By being a voter this November, you can make your voice heard on issues that will shape you and your loved ones' lives.

Some elected officials have already shown us that they will do whatever is in their power to continue rolling back our rights. So let's send a clear message that the power is in our hands and that we will use our votes and voices to create the better world we know is possible.
