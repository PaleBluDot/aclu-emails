A state's budget reflects our values and priorities. This year, the budget offers a glimpse into how New Jersey plans to chart its way forward to ensure that we can recover fully from the COVID-19 pandemic as a state.

  • Ensuring pandemic relief for all, regardless of immigration status.
  • Offering rental assistance to tenants facing eviction.
