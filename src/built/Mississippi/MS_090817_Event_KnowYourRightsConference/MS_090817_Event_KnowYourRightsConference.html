KNOW YOUR RIGHTS CONFERENCE

Do you know your rights? Living in a politically-charged, often unexpected environment, it's important to know what your rights are in protest, at the polling precinct, or even dealing with police. The ACLU of Mississippi is hosting a series of Know Your Rights (KYR) workshops for college students and community members. All workshops are free and open to the public.

Sessions will focus on freedom of speech and expression, voting, LGBTQ rights, encounters with law enforcement, and serving as legal observers. Programs will also include a town-hall style session about important local and state policy issues.

We are thrilled to partner with the Ole Miss ACLU chapter for our first program at the University of Mississippi campus in Oxford on Friday, September 15.
