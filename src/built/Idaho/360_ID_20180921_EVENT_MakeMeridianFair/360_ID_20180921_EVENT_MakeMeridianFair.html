On Tuesday, September 25, Meridian City Council could take a historical step forward in passing a non-discrimination ordinance to provide equal protections to all who call Meridian home. We can make Meridian a more fair place, but now is the time to act!

With only a few days left before the public hearing and vote, we need your voice so that each city council member clearly knows that the community is overwhelmingly in support of this ordinance!

Contact Meridian City Council and ask that they vote YES on this ordinance.

This non-discrimination city ordinance simply brings the law up-to-date, providing fair protections against discrimination for gay and transgender people. And many Idahoans agree – all hardworking people in our community, including gay and transgender people, should have the chance to earn a living and provide for themselves and their families, secure safe and stable housing, and access businesses open to the public. When everyone is treated fair, we all prosper.

Contact Meridian City Council today and ask for fair treatment for all in our community.
