I wanted to take a second to introduce myself – my name is AC Facci, and I'll be working with the advocacy team at the ACLU of Oklahoma this legislative session. Along with Nicole McAfee, our director of policy and advocacy, we'll be using this space to keep you up to date with what's been going down at 23rd and Lincoln each week.

The 2021 legislative session is going to be one of the toughest sessions we've seen for civil liberties and civil rights. We're already tracking hundreds of bills, a large number which will infringe on the civil liberties and civil rights of Oklahomans, including continuing to try to roll back popular criminal justice reforms put in place by State Question 780, policy that would limit access to the right to vote, and attacks on transgender youth and health care.

The legislative session begins on Monday, and while we know that these are big fights we're up against, I also believe deeply in the power of ACLU supporters in Oklahoma.

If you're ready to roll up your sleeves and get to work, we'd love for you to join us at one of our upcoming volunteer events:

  • Op-Ed Training.
  • Storytelling Training.
  • 2021 Lobby Week.

Links to register for all of our upcoming training can be found on our website.

Talk to you next week!
