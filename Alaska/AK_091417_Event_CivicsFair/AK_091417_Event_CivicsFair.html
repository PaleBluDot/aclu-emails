Celebrate Constitution Day

Sunday, September 17 is Constitution Day, the anniversary of the formation and signing of the U.S. Constitution in 1787! Celebrate with us at the Civics Fair this Saturday, September 16. Come hear from dynamic speakers, meet local civic organizations, and learn how to become involved in your community!

Constitution Day Civics Fair
Saturday, September 16, 1 – 3 p.m.
Z. J. Loussac Public Library
3600 Denali St.
Anchorage, AK 99503
RSVP

This event is sponsored by the ACLU of Alaska, the Municipality of Anchorage, Alaska Bar Association, Anchorage Public Library, and many other local organizations.

The Constitution is an incredibly important document. It safeguards our most cherished rights and lays out a blueprint for a government that represents the people. By getting involved, you can help make Anchorage a better place and ensure that the Constitution's protections remain strong right here at home.

Let us know you’re coming on Facebook or by sending us an email. We hope to see you on Saturday!
