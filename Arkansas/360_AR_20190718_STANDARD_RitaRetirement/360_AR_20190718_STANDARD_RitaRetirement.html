Earlier this week we announced the retirement of the ACLU of Arkansas' longtime executive director, Rita Sklar.

Sign The Card

Rita has tirelessly and tenaciously defended civil liberties in Arkansas for more than 25 years.

Under her leadership, the ACLU of Arkansas has vigorously defended the rights of students, LGBTQ people and immigrants. We've challenged racial injustice and shut down debtors' prisons. We've stopped attempts to discriminate against transgender people and ban abortion. And we've gone to court to defend freedom of speech and the separation of church and state.

Thanks in no small part to Rita's fierce determination, Arkansas is far more just, equitable and free than it was 25 years ago.

Join us in wishing her well in her retirement and thanking her for what she has accomplished on behalf of the ACLU.

Her achievements will continue to inspire us as we keep up the fight for justice — and dare to create a more perfect union.

Thanks for sticking with us,

Mike Cobb
Board President, ACLU of Arkansas
