Let's fight for a better Mississippi!

A little over a week ago, we hosted a watch party for the Let People Vote livestream launch event, hosted by People Power. Now we are ready to bring volunteers together for a call with People Power and ACLU of Mississippi staff to discuss the next steps in the Let People Vote campaign.

Join us for a webinar on Wednesday, October 11 to take the next step as we acquaint you with People Power and the Let People Vote call to action!

People Power is a special project of the ACLU, through which volunteer activists fuel action to defend our basic freedoms and hold leaders accountable. Volunteers who are part of the Let People Vote campaign will be focused on election reforms to expand access to the ballot.

In Mississippi, we need your help to convince the state legislature to authorize two weeks of early, no excuse voting and online registration. These measures will help broaden access and strengthen democracy in Mississippi.
