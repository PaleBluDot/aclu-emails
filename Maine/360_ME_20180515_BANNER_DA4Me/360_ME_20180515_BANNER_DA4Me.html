District Attorneys are the most powerful elected official you never think about. As government prosecutors, they make key decisions in criminal cases about who gets charged, what they get charged with, and the ultimate punishments they face. And beyond making decisions in individual cases, they help set the tone for how our communities respond to major societal issues.

DAs are elected officials, so they answer to you. And they're all up for election this year. Now you have the tools to learn about the candidates and to hold them accountable — all in one website.

  • Can we end our over-reliance on incarceration?
