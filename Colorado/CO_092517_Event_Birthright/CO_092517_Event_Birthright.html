BirthRight: A War Story

Join us on Wednesday, September 27 at Landmark's Esquire Theatre for a viewing of BirthRight: A War Story. Hosted by ACLU of Colorado and Colorado Religious Coalition for Reproductive Choice.

See the film that examines how women are being jailed, physically violated and even put at risk of dying as a radical movement tightens its grip across America.

Birthright: A War Story explores the accelerating gains of the crusade to control pregnant women and the fallout that is creating a public health crisis, turning pregnant women into criminals and challenging the constitutional protections of every woman in America.

Post-screening Q&A with Joyce Lisbin from Colorado Religious Coalition for Reproductive Choice and Denise Maes from ACLU of Colorado.
